## Content Pillars
Define 3–5 recurring content themes that cover both brand recognition and conversion.

| Pillar | Description | Example |
|---|---|---|
| 1. Education | Teach homeowners what to watch for | "Signs your water heater is failing" |
| 2. Behind the Job | Show the real work — time-lapses, reveals | Before/after HVAC install in Gardnerville |
| 3. Trust & Team | Faces, personality, local roots | "Meet our lead tech, [Name]" |
| 4. Seasonal Urgency | Timely prompts to act before problems hit | "Get your AC serviced before July" |
| 5. Social Proof | Reviews, happy customers, local shoutouts | Screenshot of 5-star Google review |

## Category Playbook Reference — contractors (HVAC & Plumbing)

### What Works
- Before/after project reveals (water heater swap, HVAC installs)
- Time-lapse of a job from start to finish
- "What this job actually involves" — process transparency
- Common mistakes homeowners make with HVAC/plumbing
- Answering FAQs homeowners are afraid to ask
- Showcasing the team on the job site
- Behind-the-scenes of tools, materials, craftsmanship

### Hook Ideas
- "We found this inside a wall and the homeowner had no idea..."
- "This is what a new HVAC system actually costs in Carson Valley..."
- "Don't hire anyone until you watch this first..."
- "Most homeowners wait too long on this. Here's what happens..."
- "Your water heater is telling you it's about to fail — here's the sign..."
- "We installed a smart thermostat and saved this Gardnerville family $X/month..."

### CTA Ideas
- "Get a free estimate — link in bio or call (775) 293-8855"
- "DM us a photo and we'll tell you if it's urgent"
- "We're booking summer jobs now — reach out before we fill up"
- "Comment your zip code and we'll let you know if we serve your area"

### Conversion Content That Works
- Free estimates / consultations
- Seasonal urgency ("before summer heat hits" / "winter is coming — get your furnace checked")
- Local project spotlights (Minden, Gardnerville, Carson Valley neighbors)
- Smart thermostat install promos tied to new system purchases

### Key Metrics to Watch
- Calls and form fills after video posts
- DMs asking for quotes or availability
- Saves on educational/warning content
- Shares from homeowners tagging neighbors
