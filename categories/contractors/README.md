# Contractors & Trades — Content Playbook

## What Works in This Category
- Before/after project reveals
- Time-lapse of a job from start to finish
- "What this job actually involves" — process transparency
- Common mistakes or problems homeowners make
- Answering FAQs homeowners are afraid to ask
- Showcasing the team on the job site
- Behind-the-scenes of tools, materials, craftsmanship

## Hook Ideas
- "We found this inside a wall and the homeowner had no idea..."
- "This is what a [service] actually costs in Carson Valley..."
- "Don't hire anyone until you watch this first..."
- "Most homeowners wait too long on [problem]. Here's what happens..."

## CTA Ideas
- "Get a free estimate — link in bio or call us"
- "DM us a photo and we'll tell you if it's urgent"
- "We're booking [month] jobs now — reach out before we fill up"
- "Comment your zip code and we'll let you know if we serve your area"

## Conversion Content That Works
- Free estimates / consultations
- Seasonal urgency ("before winter hits" / "summer roofing season is full")
- Referral incentives promoted through social
- Local project spotlights (neighbors see their street, their neighborhood)

## Key Metrics to Watch
- Calls and form fills after video posts
- DMs asking for quotes or availability
- Saves on educational/warning content
- Shares from homeowners tagging neighbors
