# Health & Wellness — Content Playbook

## What Works in This Category
- Transformation stories (with permission)
- "Day in the life" of a trainer, therapist, or practitioner
- Quick tip and educational content (builds authority)
- Studio or space tours
- Class previews and workout snippets
- Client testimonials and milestone moments
- Myth-busting content relevant to your niche

## Hook Ideas
- "Here's the one thing most people get wrong about [topic]..."
- "This is what a real [session/class/treatment] looks like..."
- "If you've been struggling with [pain point], watch this..."
- "Our client [first name] started [X weeks] ago. Look at her now..."

## CTA Ideas
- "Book your first session — link in bio"
- "DM us for a free consultation"
- "We have [X] spots left this week — DM to claim yours"
- "Drop a question below and we'll answer it"

## Conversion Content That Works
- Free first class / trial session offers
- Before & after (physical or emotional transformation)
- "Results in [X] weeks" style case studies
- Urgency-based promotions tied to seasonal health goals

## Key Metrics to Watch
- DMs asking about pricing, availability, and services
- Saves on educational/tip content
- Comments asking follow-up questions (high intent)
- Profile visits after testimonial or transformation content
