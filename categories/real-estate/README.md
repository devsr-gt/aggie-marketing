# Real Estate — Content Playbook

## What Works in This Category
- Property tours (walkthrough reels)
- "Just listed" and "just sold" announcements
- Carson Valley neighborhood spotlights
- Market update videos ("here's what the market is doing right now")
- Buyer/seller tips and myth-busting
- Agent "day in the life" content
- Community highlights — schools, parks, restaurants near listings

## Hook Ideas
- "This Carson Valley home just listed under [price] and here's what you get..."
- "People keep asking me if now is a good time to buy in Carson Valley..."
- "I've sold [X] homes in Gardnerville. Here's what buyers always overlook..."
- "This neighborhood doesn't get enough attention — and it's one of my favorites..."

## CTA Ideas
- "DM me 'info' to get the full listing details"
- "Thinking about buying or selling? Let's have a 15-minute call — link in bio"
- "Save this if you're planning to move to Carson Valley"
- "Comment your must-have and I'll find your match"

## Conversion Content That Works
- Free home valuation offers
- First-time buyer guides and checklists
- Neighborhood comparison content (drives DMs)
- Interest rate / market timing content (creates urgency)

## Key Metrics to Watch
- DMs from buyers and sellers asking questions
- Saves on market tip and neighborhood content
- Profile visits after listing tours
- Link-in-bio clicks to listings or booking pages
