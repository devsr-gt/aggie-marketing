# Retail — Content Playbook

## What Works in This Category
- New arrivals and unboxing content
- Styling and "how to wear/use it" videos
- Before/after: customer transformations
- "What's trending in the shop right now"
- Product storytelling — where it came from, why you carry it
- Staff picks and personal recommendations
- Shop tours and redesigns

## Hook Ideas
- "We just got these in and they're already flying off the shelf..."
- "This is the one thing every Carson Valley home needs right now..."
- "Our best seller this week and here's why people love it..."
- "You won't find this anywhere else in the valley..."

## CTA Ideas
- "Come in or DM us to hold one for you"
- "Shop the link in bio"
- "Only [X] left — DM us to grab yours"
- "Tell us which color you'd pick"

## Conversion Content That Works
- Flash sales announced social-first
- "DM us for a discount code" offers
- Loyalty program tie-ins
- Gift guide content around local holidays and events

## Key Metrics to Watch
- Link-in-bio clicks (online shop traffic)
- DMs asking about availability or sizing
- Saves (wishlist behavior)
- Shares (people tagging friends to buy together)
