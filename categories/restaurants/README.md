# Restaurants & Food — Content Playbook

## What Works in This Category
- Behind-the-scenes kitchen prep and plating
- "Day in the life" of the chef or owner
- Ingredient sourcing and farm-to-table stories
- Staff spotlights — put faces to the business
- Customer reactions and first-bite moments
- Seasonal menus and limited-time specials
- Before/after: raw ingredients to finished plate

## Hook Ideas
- "We've been making this dish the same way for [X] years..."
- "Most people don't know what goes into a [dish name]..."
- "This is what happens in our kitchen at 6am..."
- "We only make [X] of these per day — here's why..."

## CTA Ideas
- "Reserve your table — link in bio"
- "DM us to ask about today's special"
- "Tag someone you'd bring here"
- "We're open until [time] — come in tonight"

## Conversion Content That Works
- Limited-time offers exclusive to social followers
- "Show this post for a free [item]"
- Online ordering / reservation links
- Event promotion (live music nights, tasting events)

## Key Metrics to Watch
- Saves (people saving for "where to eat" later)
- Shares to Stories (word-of-mouth amplification)
- DMs asking about hours, reservations, menu
- Profile visits after food content spikes
